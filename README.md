**FreshMart AI Retention System**

The FreshMart AI Retention System is a smart retail analytics platform designed to help businesses improve customer retention through data-driven insights. The system analyzes customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and engagement data to identify customers who are likely to churn.

It provides an interactive dashboard where retail employees can monitor key business metrics such as customer retention rate, churn percentage, total customers, average basket value, and overall revenue. The platform also enables proactive engagement by allowing employees to send SMS notifications and personalized offers to high-risk customers, encouraging them to return.

Built with a React frontend, FastAPI backend, and Python-based analytics modules, the system processes customer data and presents it through intuitive visualizations and analytics. This helps retail teams make informed decisions, improve customer loyalty, and increase revenue.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how AI-driven analytics and proactive engagement strategies can help retail businesses reduce churn and achieve higher customer retention rates.
